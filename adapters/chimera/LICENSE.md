## Rationale for Dual Licensing

The CHIMERA Suite adapters are dual-licensed to maximize compatibility and reusability:

1. **Apache 2.0** provides explicit patent grants and is preferred by many organizations
2. **MIT** is simpler and more permissive, compatible with a wider range of projects
3. **Dual licensing** allows users to choose the license that best fits their needs
